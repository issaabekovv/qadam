What is IELTS?

IELTS (International English Language Testing System) is a globally recognized English proficiency exam. It evaluates your ability to communicate in English across four skills: Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking.

IELTS is required for studying abroad, international careers, and immigration to English-speaking countries.

Exam Format

Listening

Tests your understanding of spoken English through recorded conversations and lectures.

Reading

Measures comprehension and analytical skills using academic and general texts.

Writing

Evaluates your ability to organize ideas clearly in written English.

Speaking

A live interview that assesses fluency, pronunciation, and communication skills.
